# My Favorite Movie

## List of My Favorite Movie 
### -Hotel De Luna
    Hotel del Luna is a 2019 South Korean fantasy romance drama about a mystical hotel for ghosts, owned by the immortal Jang Man-wol, who is cursed to run it for over 1,000 years. The story follows her and her new, human manager, Gu Chan-sung, as they help restless souls resolve unfinished business before they can move on to the afterlife. The series is known for its unique premise, stunning visuals, and a central love story that explores themes of redemption, love, and letting go. 

    
### -The Notebook
      The Notebook is a romantic drama about a young couple from different social classes in the 1940s, Noah and Allie, who fall deeply in love one summer but are separated by her parents. Years later, they reunite and must navigate their rekindled passion, while the story is framed by an elderly man reading from a notebook to a woman in a nursing home, revealing their full story. The film explores themes of enduring love, memory, and devotion across a lifetime.
